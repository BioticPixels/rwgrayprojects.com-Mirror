Tedlar® Adhesives


Adhesives

    Product Description:

    A line of heat activated acrylic adhesive products with long-term exterior durability.


    Uses:

Tedlar® Film


Polyvinyl Fluoride Film

    Product Description:

    A durable, chemically inert, pre-formed polyvinyl fluoride film manufactured in continuous rolls.


    Uses:

  • Transportation:
  • Airline and ground-transit interiors
  • Autobody trim
  • Exterior surfacing for truck panels
  • Building products:
  • Stain-resistant wall coverings
  • Insulation barrier
  • Protective surface for fiberglass panels
  • Exterior surface protection for vinyl siding and vinyl fabrics
  • Release film for:
  • Printed circuit boards
  • Epoxies
  • Phenolics
  • Polyesters
  • Rubber compounds
  • Decorative applications:
  • Protective surfacing for decals or labels
  • Protective backing for photovoltaic cells
  • Protective surfacing for backlit signs and awnings
