Build-up of the 120 Polyhedron

People have asked me to show them how all the polyhedra within the 120 Polyhedron fit within the 120 polyhedron. Although I have tried to give a sense of this in my paper "What's In This Polyhedron", I did not try to show each polyhedron in the 120 Polyhedron.

So, here is a pictorial presentation of all the polyhedra within the 120 Polyhedron.



Here is the 120 Polyhedron. It has 120 triangular faces.

The 120 Polyhedron

Here are all the other polyhedra.

5 Cubes
5 Octahedra
5 Rhombic Dodecahedra
1 Regular Dodecahedron
