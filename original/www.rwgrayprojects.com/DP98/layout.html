Dome Strut Layout

This dome is a 30 foot diameter, 4-frequency, 1/2 sphere, Icosahedron based dome.

There are 5 different strut lengths.

There are 60 A struts, 30 B struts, 40 C struts, 70 D struts and 30 E struts.
